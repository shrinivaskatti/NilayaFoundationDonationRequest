Summary of proposed scholarship for current academic year(2021-22) is as below.
 
Every year more & more applications are received for scholarships with increased numbers of deserving applications. This year a major portion of applicants are impacted by COVID 19 pandemic & related hardships.

Considering the delays in academic sessions/ admissions this year due to COVID, Nilaya Foundation is working  for the Scholarship program in two phases.

Phase1 : (Applications in this phase have been received by 15 Sept 2021)
700+ applications were received from scholarship aspirants. Evaluated these and identified 198 eligible, needy and deserving students for award of Niaya Foundation Scholarships. Total scholarship to be awarded in Phase 1 is ~23 Lakhs.

Please click here for a list of students considered under this scheme. Like last year, we have identified ~15 very special students who need complete sponsorship of their education.

In order to ensure really deserving candidates are identified, we are doing further background verification through personal visits to ~82 students in the next 2-3 weeks.  We will keep you updated if any changes in the shortlisted student list or amount. 


Phase2 :(Applications in this phase will be commencing from 1st week of Nov 2021)
In this phase we will call for applications from students who got admission after Sept 15, We will review all these applications and add them to the list in Nov last week. We expect to add another 30-40 students (~7L scholarship).


Sustained support and generous donations year on year from you have made us take another step ahead this year. We felt the need to revise and increase the number of students and with the help of donors like you, we are sure of making this happen.
